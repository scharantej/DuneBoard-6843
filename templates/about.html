Dune: Imperium

About the Game

Overview

Dune: Imperium is a worker placement and deckbuilding game where players compete to control the planet of Dune. The game is based on the popular Dune novels by Frank Herbert and is designed by Paul Dennen.

In Dune: Imperium, players take on the role of one of four factions: the Atreides, the Harkonnen, the Fremen, or the Bene Gesserit. Each faction has its own unique abilities and strengths, and players must use these to their advantage to gain control of the planet.

The game is played over a series of rounds, and in each round, players take turns placing their workers on different spaces on the game board. These spaces allow players to collect resources, build armies, and take other actions to help them achieve their goals.

At the end of each round, players score points based on the amount of control they have over different regions of the planet. The player with the most points at the end of the game wins.
